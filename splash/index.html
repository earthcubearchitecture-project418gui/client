Geoscience Cyberinfrastructure for Open Discovery in the Earth Sciences

Want to share your resources with the geoscience community?

We’ve made it easy!

Simply enter your metadata in our wizard-based tool to quickly convert it to JSON-LD and the GeoCODES metadata recommendation based on the Schema.org vocabulary with geoscience-specific extensions. By publishing the resulting JSON-LD on your website, your datasets and organizations will be findable and accessible through GeoCODES and the Google Dataset Search tool.
